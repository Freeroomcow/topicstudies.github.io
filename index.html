Retiring

Consider this my official retirement from working on the website. I will keep it up for obvious reasons but I will not be ACTIVELY working on it anymore. Notice actively because I will still add a game from time to time but definetly not as often. Sorry to you guys for ending the devlopement so early but I feel like the website has enough to hold anyone over until summer hits. If I feel compelled enough I could possibly resume devlopement in the future. As always my full source code is available and I give anyone rights to make their own forks of the website and add whatever they want but I just want it known that topicstudies.github.io is my only official domain and I do not associate myself with any website that is a fork of my own.
